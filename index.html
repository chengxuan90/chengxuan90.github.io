Biography:

I am currently an associate researcher/ assistant professor at the Department of Software Engineering, School of Informatics, Xiamen University. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in the State Key Lab of CAD&CG at Zhejiang University. I was an research intern at Microsoft Research Asia and a visting scholar in City University of Hong Kong. My general research interests cover the broad area of computer graphics, 3D vision and multimedia, with special emphasis on 3D reconstruction, image generation and geometry processing.
程轩，厦门大学信息学院软件工程系特任副研究员/助理教授，硕士生导师，IEEE/CCF/CSIG会员。 2016年博士毕业于浙江大学计算机辅助设计与图形学国家重点实验室，曾在微软亚洲研究院和香港城市大学学术访问。 主要研究领域为计算机图形学、3D视觉和多媒体，尤其是三维重建、图像生成、几何处理等。 主持国家自然科学基金青年项目、福建省自然科学基金项目、厦门市自然科学基金项目和多项企业横向课题。 获得2013陆增镛CAD&CG高科技奖三等奖、2021厦门市高层次人才、2022福建省高层次人才等荣誉。
